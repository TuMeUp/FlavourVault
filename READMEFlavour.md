# FlavourVault
A digital home for flavours, memories, and the recipes that bring people together.

FlavourVault is a growing archive built to honour the meals that shaped us, the stories carried through food, and the knowledge passed down through families and communities. It treats recipes not just as instructions, but as pieces of identity, history, and connection.

# Purpose
FlavourVault exists to:

Preserve meaningful recipes alongside the memories that give them life

Protect cultural and family knowledge so it can be carried forward

Create a space where food, story, and identity can be organised and remembered

Support connection across generations through shared flavours and experiences
