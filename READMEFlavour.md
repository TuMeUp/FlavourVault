# FlavourVault

A digital home for flavours, memories, and the recipes that bring people together.

FlavourVault is a personal recipe book designed to capture your favourite meals, family traditions, and culinary experiments. Store, organize, and share your recipes in one beautiful, easy-to-use digital space.

## Features
- Add and edit recipes
- Organize by categories, ingredients, or tags
- Save memories and notes with each recipe
- Share recipes with friends and family

## Getting Started
1. Clone this repository: `git clone https://github.com/yourusername/FlavourVault.git`
2. Explore, add, and manage your recipes
3. Contribute to improving the app and its features

## Contributing
Contributions are welcome! Feel free to submit issues or pull requests to enhance FlavourVault.
